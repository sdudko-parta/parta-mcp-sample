# Tools and Equipment

You do not need a fully stocked bakery to make a great cake. Start with the essentials and add specialty tools only when a recipe calls for one.

## What you actually need

- **A digital scale.** The single biggest upgrade to your baking. Measuring by weight is faster and more accurate than cups.
- **Two mixing bowls.** One for dry ingredients, one for wet.
- **A whisk and a flexible spatula.** A whisk for aerating; a spatula for folding and scraping.
- **A 9-inch round or 8-inch square cake pan.** Light-colored aluminum bakes more evenly than dark non-stick.
- **Parchment paper.** Lines the pan so the cake releases cleanly.
- **An oven thermometer.** Most home ovens are off by 15 to 25 degrees. This tells you the truth.

## Nice to have, not required

- A stand mixer or hand mixer (a whisk and a bowl will do the same job with more effort).
- An offset spatula for frosting.
- A cake tester or a thin skewer.
