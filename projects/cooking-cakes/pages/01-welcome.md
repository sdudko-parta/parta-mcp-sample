# Welcome to Cake Baking

Cakes are one of the most rewarding things you can bake. They look impressive, smell incredible, and turn a regular afternoon into an event.

## What you will learn

- The role each ingredient plays in a cake
- The tools that actually matter (and the ones you can skip)
- The mixing techniques that decide whether your crumb is light or dense
- A simple vanilla sponge recipe you can repeat from memory

## Who this is for

You have never baked a cake from scratch, or you have tried and ended up with something flat, dry, or rubbery. By the end of this course, you will know why that happened and how to avoid it.
